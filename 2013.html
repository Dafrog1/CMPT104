Faker Takes Center Stage

Going into the 2013 world chapionship SKT T1 did have some hype around them especcially star rookie midlaner Faker. Groupstage would see SKT only drop 1 game to chinese representatives OMG Although they would also claim a win over the chinese team. In the playoff bracket t1 would sweep over Taiwans Gama Bears play a tight series against regional rivals Najin Sword. SKT would match up against China's Royal Club the tournament favoriets in the grand finals and would sweep aside the lpl representatives earning the organization its first world title.

The 2013 rooster was built up of talented rookies and designed to play around solo que star and Midlaner Faker. after the team strugled in 2014 toplaner Impact would go on to become a star player in North America ADC Piglet would follow with mixed success while support PoohManDu would take a trip to Europe. Bengi and Faker would be the only hold outs of the roster.

Faker would be seen as the star he was and claimed the title as best midlaner in the world from the English Casters. This tournament would establish Korea as a higly competative force on the International scene.
